1) Masala Dosa

Origin: Karnataka A crispy fermented rice–lentil crepe filled with spiced potato masala.

Ingredients

For Dosa Batter:
2 cups raw rice
½ cup urad dal (split black gram)
1 tsp fenugreek seeds
Salt to taste
Water as needed

For Potato Masala: 3 boiled potatoes (mashed) 1 onion (thinly sliced) 2 green chilies (chopped) 1 tsp mustard seeds 1 tsp urad dal 8–10 curry leaves ¼ tsp turmeric powder 1 tbsp oil Salt to taste Preparation Step 1: Prepare Batter Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4–6 hours. Grind to a smooth batter (slightly thick). Mix well, add salt, and ferment overnight (8–10 hours). Step 2: Prepare Potato Masala Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds; let them splutter. Add urad dal and curry leaves. Add onions and green chilies; sauté till soft. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes. Add salt and mix well. Cook for 2–3 minutes. Step 3: Make Dosa Heat a flat tawa (griddle). Pour a ladle of batter and spread thinly in a circular motion. Drizzle oil around edges. Cook until golden and crisp. Place potato masala in the center and fold. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
